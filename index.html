About Our Club

Discover what makes Pulari Club special

Connecting Community Through Arts & Sports

Pulari Arts and Sports Club was founded with a vision to create a vibrant community space where people of all ages can explore their creativity and athletic potential. We believe in the holistic development of individuals through a balanced approach to arts and physical activities.

Over the years, we've organized numerous football tournaments, art exhibitions, dance workshops, and community programs that have brought together thousands of participants and spectators.
